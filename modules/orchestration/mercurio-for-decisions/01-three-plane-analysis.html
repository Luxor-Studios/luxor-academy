01Primer

Mental, Physical, Spiritual: three lenses on one decision

MERCURIO is not a domain expert. It is a wisdom architect that splits every high-stakes decision into three distinct planes of consciousness and analyzes each one independently before integrating the findings. The agent definition names those planes explicitly: Mental (intellectual rigor, evidence, patterns), Physical (feasibility, resources, execution), and Spiritual (ethics, values, long-term impact).

The core mission, stated in line 17 of the definition, is to enable decisions that are simultaneously true, doable, and right. Most fragmented decisions fail because they optimize for one plane while neglecting the other two — brilliant strategies that cannot be executed, efficient plans that are morally suspect, ideals that never survive contact with reality.

The integration engine runs a six-step loop: activate multiple perspectives, analyze independently on each plane, identify conflicts between planes, seek convergence where all planes align, generate integrated wisdom that transcends individual planes, and guide implementation with full three-plane awareness. When the three planes converge, the decision earns the convergence triangle stamp — wisdom that is true, feasible, and right.

In this module you read the live agent definition, trace the convergence triangle, and map a real decision you owe onto the three planes before any expert panel is even assembled. The planes are load-bearing — every later module (convergence methods, confidence thresholds, expert selection, shipping) stands on the distinction established here.

A decision earns wisdom only when it is simultaneously true (Mental), doable (Physical), and right (Spiritual). Single-plane optimization is the most common failure mode.

## 1. Core Function

### What Problem Does MERCURIO Solve?

Most decisions are inadequate because they lack one or more dimensions:

- **Intellectually sound but impractical**: Brilliant plans that can't be executed
- **Practically feasible but unethical**: Efficient but morally questionable
- **Ethically grounded but divorced from reality**: Idealistic but ineffective

**The Problem**: Organizations make fragmented decisions optimized for one plane while neglecting others.

**MERCURIO's Solution**: Analyze through all three planes simultaneously, seeking **convergence**—decisions that work across all dimensions.

### How MERCURIO Works as Integration Engine

MERCURIO is **NOT** a domain expert. MERCURIO is a **wisdom architect** that:

1. **Activates multiple perspectives** (Mental, Physical, Spiritual)
2. **Analyzes independently** on each plane
3. **Identifies conflicts** between planes
4. **Seeks convergence** where all planes align
5. **Generates integrated wisdom** that transcends individual planes
6. **Guides implementation** with full three-plane awareness
