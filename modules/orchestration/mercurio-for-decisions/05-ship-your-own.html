01Primer

Frame a decision you actually owe, then run the loop end-to-end

You have read the three planes, matched decision shapes to convergence methods, calibrated the six confidence bands, and assembled a 3-to-7-expert panel that covers every dimension without paying prohibitive coordination cost. This module is where the reading stops and the shipping starts.

The canonical MERCURIO orchestrator file is explicit about the output artifact: a NOUS.md — described in the orchestrator as a living map of synthesized knowledge that is built and maintained, not written-once-and-forgotten. The orchestrator's Mental Plane directive is to synthesize information from multiple sources into coherent, actionable wisdom, document your research in NOUS.md with clear structure, and always cite sources and acknowledge uncertainty where it exists. That last clause is what separates a decision document from a pitch deck: calibrated MERCURIO output is allowed to say we do not know yet.

The Physical Plane directive from the same orchestrator file adds the operational layer: continuously assess what you know versus what you need to know, be proactive in identifying gaps and taking action to fill them, and maintain clear vision of ultimate goals while handling immediate needs. A shipped decision is one where the gaps are named, the next actions are assigned, and the confidence band is stamped at the top of the document.

In this module you frame one real decision you owe — a hiring call, an architecture pick, a pricing move, a product kill — and run the full MoE loop: three planes, panel assembly, method pick, threshold calibration, and NOUS-style convergence document. You paste the draft into the form and queue it for the Academy pipeline. Phase 1 vests 150 XP the moment you queue; Phase 3 mints the permalink that turns your decision document into a teachable specimen another learner can study.

A shipped MERCURIO decision is a NOUS.md — a living document that cites its sources, names its gaps, stamps its confidence band, and assigns next actions. Pitch decks are allowed to hide uncertainty; convergence documents are not.

05Self-Check

2 — The Mental Plane bullet list in the orchestrator file ends with a specific discipline that separates an honest decision document from a pitch. Which phrase appears verbatim?
Line 29: "Always cite sources and acknowledge uncertainty where it exists." The licence to say "we do not know yet" is what the orchestrator explicitly grants — and what pitch decks reflexively strip out.
3 — Why is the canonical last module of every LUXOR Academy Quest a "Ship Your Own" step rather than a summary quiz?
Sovereignty. Per §13 of the Academy meta-prompt and the MERCURIO canonical-last-module pattern, learners finish every Quest holding a permalink to their own artifact, not a score. The Quest trains you for the thing, not for the Quest.
