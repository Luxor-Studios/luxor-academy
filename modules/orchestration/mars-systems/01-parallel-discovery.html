01Primer

Fire three specialists at once

MARS is not a domain expert. Its own agent definition is blunt about it: MARS is NOT a domain expert (that's what specialized agents are for). MARS is a meta-cognitive orchestrator. What MARS does is decompose a complex problem into research-able domains and then dispatch specialists in parallel — each with a scoped budget of roughly five thousand tokens and an extraction target of four hundred to five hundred focused words.

The shape of a MARS research round is load-bearing. Phase 1 (problem decomposition) names the constituent domains — logistics, technology, people, finance, whatever the problem actually contains — and maps which depend on which. Phase 2 (parallel discovery) launches N specialists simultaneously. Each returns a focused extract, not a monograph. The total wall-clock cost is Max specialist (not sum), typically 3-5 min, while the total token cost is the sum of the fan-out, around twenty-five thousand for five domains.

That arithmetic is the whole point. Sequential research costs you the sum of times and the sum of tokens. Parallel research costs you the sum of tokens but only the maximum of times. On a five-domain problem that is a 5× wall-clock win for the same token spend — which is exactly the SpaceX-level innovation on the ground in all moments tempo MARS exists to enable.

Before you can synthesize anything across domains, you need the fan-out to have happened cleanly. That is Module 01.

Parallel discovery costs the sum of tokens but only the max of wall-clock times. Five domains in parallel ship in the time of one.

### Mode 1: Research Mode (Parallel Discovery)

**When to Use**: Need comprehensive understanding across multiple domains

**How It Works**:
```
MARS orchestrates N specialists in parallel:
├─ Specialist 1 researches Domain 1 (budget: 5K)
├─ Specialist 2 researches Domain 2 (budget: 5K)
├─ Specialist 3 researches Domain 3 (budget: 5K)
└─ Specialist N researches Domain N (budget: 5K)

Each produces focused extract (400-500 words)
Total time: Max specialist (not sum), typically 3-5 min
Total tokens: Sum of specialists (~25K for 5 domains)
```
