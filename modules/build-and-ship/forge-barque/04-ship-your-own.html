01Primer

Run the pipeline on your own repo

You have just read how BARQUE activates its venv, themes its CSS, and wires its email. That pattern generalises. Any repo that converts Markdown (or YAML, or JSON) into a rendered artifact and ships it somewhere is fair game for the same treatment.

This module is the canonical last module of every LUXOR Academy Quest. You paste a URL to your own repo — your report generator, your invoice renderer, your daily-digest bot, your static-site builder — and the Academy's codebase-to-course pipeline extracts the same six slots from your code: a primer anchored in your README, a visual traced from your architecture, an interactive sandbox pointed at your real CLI, an artifact deep-link into your canonical file, a self-check derived from your tests, and a next-step that routes a future learner through your codebase.

What you get back is a permalink. Not a certificate. Not a badge. A URL that teaches someone else the thing you just built, with your name on the attribution line. The economy inverts: by completing this Quest you have not only learned BARQUE's shape, you have produced a teachable specimen of your own. That is the sovereignty clause — you own your progress, and you own the Quest your progress generated.

Phase 1 of LUXOR Academy captures your repo URL and queues it for the pipeline. In Phase 3 the pipeline runs live and returns a draft .module.json that you can review, edit, and publish.

Every Quest ends the same way — you put your own repo through the same contract and walk away with a permalink quest that teaches what you built.

### Basic Usage

```bash
# Initialize BARQUE in your project
barque init

# Generate PDF from single markdown file
barque generate document.md

# Generate both light and dark themes
barque generate document.md --theme both

# Generate and send via email
barque send report.md --to boss@company.com

# Process entire directory
barque batch docs/ --output pdfs/

# Send existing PDF file
barque email report.pdf --to client@example.com --subject "Monthly Report"
```
